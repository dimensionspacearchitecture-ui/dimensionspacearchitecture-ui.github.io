About Dimension Space Architecture

“The ultimate aim of architecture is to create a place where people feel at home.” — Alvar Aalto

Dimension Space Architecture is a boutique architectural design practice based in Mount Maunganui, New Zealand, founded by Phil Austen.

With many years of experience in architecture, the practice focuses on creating bespoke residential, multi-unit, and light commercial spaces that are carefully tailored to the people who inhabit them. Each project begins with listening — understanding how a space should feel, function, and evolve over time.

Dimension Space Architecture approaches design with a strong emphasis on spatial clarity, materiality, and environmental response. Particular care is given to material selection, ensuring finishes are not only visually refined but also durable and appropriate for their setting. This is especially important in coastal and high-exposure environments, where climate, salt air, and sun significantly influence how buildings perform and age.

Phil Austen lives in the Bay of Plenty with his wife, Alex, and has called the region home for over a decade. Having designed, built, and lived in multiple homes locally, he brings first-hand understanding of the region’s climate and the complexities involved in delivering well-considered architecture in this environment.

The practice provides clear guidance through New Zealand building code requirements, planning controls, and consent processes, helping clients navigate what can often feel like a complex regulatory landscape. To support informed decision-making, 3D visualisation and virtual walkthroughs are offered, allowing clients to experience their space before construction begins.

Dimension Space Architecture also assists clients considering secondary dwellings or granny flats, including advice on upcoming legislation expected in 2026, which will allow for unconsented granny flats of up to 70m², subject to specific criteria. The practice can assist with design, planning considerations, and compliance requirements to ensure these additions integrate seamlessly with existing homes.

Services

  • Bespoke residential design
  • Multi-unit and medium-density design
  • Light commercial design
  • Masterplanning
  • Interior design and material selection
  • 3D rendering and virtual walkthroughs
  • Resource and building consent documentation

At Dimension Space Architecture, the focus is on creating calm, considered spaces that respond to their environment and reflect the people who live within them.
